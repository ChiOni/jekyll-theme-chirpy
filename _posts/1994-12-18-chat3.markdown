#### **연습가이드 #1 | Introduction** <a name="1"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.18**
>
> 말로 문장을 만들 때, 시제를 크게 중요하게 고려하지 않고 뱉어버릴 때가 많다.  
>
> 특히 나의 경우 be going to ~ 표현이 입 밖으로 잘 나오지 않는다.  
>
> > **(예제)**
> >
> > - I usually get up at 7
> >
> > - Today I got up at 8
> >
> > - Tomorrow I'm going to get up at 6
>
> Today라는 단어와 과거형의 표현을 함께 쓰는게 아직은 좀 어색하다.
>
> > **(실습)**
> >
> > I usually eat cereal for breakfast. But I ate rice and stew this morning.  
> >
> > Tomorrow I'm not going to eat breakfast beacuase I'm goint to get up early.

#### **연습가이드 #2 | ~하려고 했는데 ~하게 됐다** <a name="2"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.19**
>
> 단어를 외울때에는 어울리는 문맥을 같이 이해해야 한다.
>
> > **(예제 #1)**
> >
> > I was actually going to go to the movies with some friends and we were going to have lunch toghether but one of them suddenly had something to do so we just eneded up canceling the whole thing.
>
> - **was/were going to go**: 원래는
> - **suddenly had something to do** 
> - **eneded up ~ing**: 결국 ~하게 됬다. 
>
> > **(예제 #2)**
> >
> > I actually wanted to buy a higher model, but I didn't have enough money. So, I ended up buying this one.
>
> - **I actually wanted to:** 원래는
>
> > **(예제 #3)**
> >
> > We were actually planning to travel abroad but we couldn't get tickets so eventually we couldn't go anywhere.
>
> - **was/were planning to:** 원래는 
> - **eventually**: 결국은 <-> finally: 드디어
>
> > **(예제 #4)**
> >
> > I really wasn't going to eat any desert but the cake looked so good. I couldn't resist. I eneded up eating 3 pieces.
> >
> > **(예제 #5)**
> >
> > I wasn't going to say anything but since you bring it up, I have something I want to ask you.
>
> - **but since you bring it up:** 얘기를 꺼내서 말인데
>
> > **(실습)**
> >
> > I was actually going to enroll in an academy for English conversation, but the youtube channel live academy looked so good, so I ended up subscribing this channel for English conversation.
