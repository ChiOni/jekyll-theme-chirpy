#### **연습가이드 #1 | Introduction** <a name="1"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.18**
>
> 말로 문장을 만들 때, 시제를 크게 중요하게 고려하지 않고 뱉어버릴 때가 많다.  
>
> 특히 나의 경우 be going to ~ 표현이 입 밖으로 잘 나오지 않는다.  
>
> > **(예제)**
> >
> > - I usually get up at 7
> >
> > - Today I got up at 8
> >
> > - Tomorrow I'm going to get up at 6
>
> Today라는 단어와 과거형의 표현을 함께 쓰는게 아직은 좀 어색하다.
>
> > **(실습)**
> >
> > I usually eat cereal for breakfast. But I ate rice and stew this morning.  
> >
> > Tomorrow I'm not going to eat breakfast beacuase I'm goint to get up early.

#### **연습가이드 #2 | ~하려고 했는데 ~하게 됐다** <a name="2"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.19**
>
> 단어를 외울때에는 어울리는 문맥을 같이 이해해야 한다.
>
> > **(예제 #1)**
> >
> > I was actually going to go to the movies with some friends and we were going to have lunch toghether but one of them suddenly had something to do so we just ended up canceling the whole thing.
>
> - **was/were going to go**: 원래는
> - **suddenly had something to do** 
> - **ended up ~ing**: 결국 ~하게 됬다. 
>
> > **(예제 #2)**
> >
> > I actually wanted to buy a higher model, but I didn't have enough money. So, I ended up buying this one.
>
> - **I actually wanted to:** 원래는
>
> > **(예제 #3)**
> >
> > We were actually planning to travel abroad but we couldn't get tickets so eventually we couldn't go anywhere.
>
> - **was/were planning to:** 원래는 
> - **eventually**: 결국은 <-> finally: 드디어
>
> > **(예제 #4)**
> >
> > I really wasn't going to eat any desert but the cake looked so good. I couldn't resist. I eneded up eating 3 pieces.
>
> <br/>
>
> > **(예제 #5)**
> >
> > I wasn't going to say anything but since you bring it up, I have something I want to ask you.
>
> - **but since you bring it up:** 얘기를 꺼내서 말인데
>
> > **(실습)**
> >
> > I was actually going to enroll in an academy for English conversation, but the youtube channel live academy looked so good, so I ended up subscribing this channel for English conversation.

#### **연습가이드 #3 | 기본 시간표현 + 시제** <a name="3"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.25**
>
> **시제 (TENSE)에 대한** **Exercise Routine**
>
> 시제에 대한 멋진 표현들을 문법적으로는 익숙하게 받아들임에도 실제 회화에서는 적용하지 못하는 경우가 많다. 시제를 암기한다기보다는 연습할 수 있는 문장들을 통으로 외우도록 하자.
>
> 
>
> > **(예제 #1)**
> >
> > I **used to** eat only pepperoni pizza.
> >
> > But one day, a friend **said that I should** try a Hawaiian pizza.
> >
> > And so I did. And I liked it! It was really good!
> >
> > So, I **started eating** Hawaiian pizzas much more often.
> >
> > And **since then**, whenever I want a pizza,
> >
> > **I've been getting** the Hawaiian rather than the pepperoni.
> >
> > And **nowadays,** I only eat Hawaiian. I can't eat pizza without pineapple toppings. 
>
> - **used to:** 예전에 즐겨했다.
> - **started ~ing:** ~하기 시작했다
> - **nowaydays:** 요즘에는 
>
> 
>
> > **(실습)**
> >
> > I used to enjoy sweet drink at cafes.
> >
> > But one day, a friend said that sweet drinks does not feel fresh at all. And so I did.
> >
> > So, I started drinking americano much more often.
> >
> > And since then, whenever I go to cafes, I've been drinknig americano rather than the sweet drink.
> >
> > And nowadays, I only drink americano. I can't enjoy drink with sweet taste.

#### **연습가이드 #5 | 일상적으로 말하기** <a name="5"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.30**
>
> 이해는 한 번만! 연습은 백 번만!
>
> >  **(예제 #1)**
> >
> > Yesterday, I went to the department store. There was **a coat that I really liked.**
> >
> > The clerk **said that** it was the last one. **I could've just bought it** **right away.**
> >
> > **But I wanted to see if** there was anything better. 
> >
> > But I couldn't find anything better. 
> >
> > But **by the time** I went back to ther first store, 
> >
> > someone had already purchased that coat.
> >
> > I should have bought it when I had the chance.
>
> - **I could have just bought it right away:** 바로 ~ 할 수도 있었다
> - **I wanted to see if:** 혹시 ~하지 않을지 보고 싶었다
> - **by the time:** ~ 했을 때는

#### **연습가이드 #6 | must,might,could have** <a name="6"></a>

> **CreateDate 2020.10.31**
>
> > **(예제 #1)**
> >
> > One of my friends recently quit his job.
> >
> >  I don't know, he **must have had** some problems with his boss.
> >
> > Either that or, he **might have had** some health problems.
> >
> > Either way, it **couldn't have been** because he was sick and tired of the job.
>
> - **must have** (강한 가정)
>   1. ~ 했나봐
>   2. ~ 했던 모양이야 - you must have been hungry
>   3.  **~ 했겠다** - you must have been really frustrated
> - **might have** (약한 가정)
>   1. 했(었)을 수도 있다
>   2. 했던 것이었을 수도 있다
